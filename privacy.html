Privacy Policy

NovaVector Holdings LLC · Effective July 3, 2026

The short version: this site uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking pixels. The only personal information we collect is an email address you choose to give us, and the only thing we do with it is send you occasional updates. You can leave the list anytime.

Who we are

NovaVector Holdings LLC is a Georgia (USA) limited liability company. This policy covers the website novavectorholdings.com. You can reach us at social@novavectorholdings.com.

What we collect

One thing, and only if you submit it: the email address you enter in the "Stay Informed" form. We do not collect names, payment details, location data, or anything else through this site. If you email us directly, we receive whatever you choose to include in your message.

What we don't do

  • No cookies and no browser tracking of any kind.
  • No analytics, advertising pixels, or fingerprinting scripts.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of your email address with third parties for their marketing.

How we use your email

To send occasional updates about NovaVector products and launches. That's it. We don't use it for anything else without asking you first.

Where it's stored

Subscriber emails are stored with Supabase, our database provider, and this site is hosted on Vercel. Like most websites, our hosting provider may briefly process standard technical request data (such as IP addresses) to serve pages and prevent abuse. Fonts are delivered by Google Fonts, which involves a standard content request to Google's servers when the page loads.

Children's privacy

This website is a general-audience corporate site and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and no part of this site is designed to do so. Product pages describing KNOVA Quest, a learning product in development, intentionally contain no data-collection features.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe: email social@novavectorholdings.com with "unsubscribe" and we'll remove you promptly.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your email address entirely and we will.
  • Questions: same address — a person reads it.

Changes to this policy

If our data practices change, we'll update this page and its effective date before the change takes effect.
